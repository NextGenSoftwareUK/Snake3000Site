# Snake 3000 — Where It All Began

**OASIS Omniverse · The Original OASIS Game**

Snake 3000 is the game where it all began — the very first OASIS-integrated title and the seed from which the entire OASIS Omniverse grew. A modern reimagining of the classic Snake game with neon green pixel aesthetics, power-ups, global leaderboards and full OASIS karma integration.

## The Story

Snake 3000 was the proof-of-concept that demonstrated the OASIS Omniverse vision: a simple game, connected to a universal karma system, showing that any app — no matter how small — can be part of something much bigger. The idea that even a game of Snake could contribute to a global karma economy became the founding principle of the entire platform.

## Features

- **Classic Snake gameplay** — grow your snake, don't hit the walls or yourself
- **Power-ups** — speed boosts, shield bubbles, multiplier pellets and portals
- **Difficulty modes** — Casual to Snake God
- **Global leaderboard** — OASIS avatar-linked, persistent across sessions
- **Karma rewards** — every high score milestone earns OASIS karma
- **Pixel grid aesthetic** — retro green-on-black with modern glow effects
